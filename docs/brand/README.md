All SVG. The monogram is a top-down coffee cup with deep teal steam — terracotta + teal is our signature contrast.

## Concept

The monogram reads as a fresh cup of coffee from above:
- **Outer ring**: saucer rim
- **Terracotta middle**: cup wall (sunrise gradient `#F4A261 → #C2410C`)
- **Espresso center**: brewed coffee
- **Cream highlight**: crema, half-elliptical reflection
- **Three teal strands**: steam (the only cool tone — anchors the warm primary, signals "morning, calm")

The wordmark uses Sora 700 for "Cup" and "Co", with an italic deep-teal `&` as the punchline.

## Tagline

**Your morning, handled.**

Set in 11px tracking-2 uppercase, used under the wordmark in marketing.

## Don't

- Don't recolor the steam to orange — the teal-against-warm is the entire point.
- Don't put the monogram on a dark background without testing legibility (the espresso center disappears).
- Don't render the wordmark below 96px wide — drop to monogram-only at small sizes.
